# PAR-Tee Pricing for Courses

## Overview

PAR-Tee is **100% free for golfers**. Courses pay a flat per-booking fee, invoiced monthly via Stripe. There are no setup fees, no monthly subscriptions, and no lock-in contracts.

---

## Standard Rate

**$2.75 per completed booking**

You're only charged for bookings that are completed — no charge for cancellations or no-shows.

---

## Early Adopter Discount Tiers

Courses that actively promote PAR-Tee to their golfers unlock reduced rates. The more you promote, the less you pay per booking. Rates are locked in permanently once earned.

| What you do | Your rate |
|-------------|-----------|
| Add PAR-Tee to your website or booking page | **$2.25/booking** |
| Actively push golfers to book through PAR-Tee (signage, staff mentions) | **$2.00/booking** |
| Run a mini tournament through PAR-Tee + promote on your socials | **$1.75/booking** |
| **Founding course** (among the first courses to sign up — locked in forever) | **$1.50/booking** |

> **Tiers are cumulative and permanent.** Once your course qualifies for a tier, your rate is locked in at that level and will never go up — even as PAR-Tee grows.

---

## Billing & Invoicing

- Invoices are generated **monthly** based on completed bookings.
- Payment is processed via **Stripe**. You'll provide bank or card details when your course signs up.
- You can view all billing history and download invoices from your **Manager Dashboard**.

---

## How Golfers Pay

PAR-Tee never handles golfer payments. Golfers pay your course directly — using whatever process you already have (at the pro shop, pre-paid online, etc.). PAR-Tee simply connects you with the golfer and handles the booking.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

**When am I charged — at booking or at play?**
You're charged per **completed booking**, meaning the golfer showed up and played. Cancellations are not billed.

**How do I qualify for the $2.25 tier?**
Add a link to PAR-Tee on your website or booking page and notify your PAR-Tee account manager. Once verified, your rate adjusts on the next invoice.

**What counts as "actively pushing golfers to book through PAR-Tee"?**
Physical signage at your course (e.g., banners or counter cards) and staff actively mentioning PAR-Tee when golfers call in or walk up to book. Reach out to your account manager to verify and claim this tier.

**Is the founding course rate still available?**
The founding course rate ($1.50/booking) is only available to early adopters — spots are limited. Contact us to find out if it's still available.

**What happens if I stop promoting PAR-Tee after locking in a discount tier?**
Your rate is locked in permanently regardless of your ongoing promotion level. We honor the commitment you made.

**Is there a minimum booking volume?**
No minimum. You pay only for what you use.
